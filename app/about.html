Care… Since 1975

From humble beginnings, our Founder Dr Damodarasamy Naidu laid the foundation of Sree Annapoorna Foods in 1975.

When many believed that good taste can be achieved only when masalas are prepared at home, our company pioneered the concept of pre-made spice blends. Inspired by the traditional practise of preparing regional spices synonymous with our cultural heritage, we take great care to source the highest quality ingredients from India’s renowned spice lands. Hand-crafted with love and without compromising on quality and hygiene, we became forerunners in the industry of processed and packaged masalas.

The company was succeeded by Mr. V Vijay Prasad, a Food Scientist and an ardent foodie at heart who relentlessly pursued the dynamics of food as an art and science. He had an unbounded passion for replicating the flavours of regional delicacies from across the country and making them available to every community and city. After a rebranding initiative was realized, the company came to be known as Annapoorna Masalas & Spices.

The special blends feature pure spices, regional blends, and biryani variants that have captured the authenticity and cultural heritage of cuisines prepared in households across the country. Our products will take your tastebuds on a culinary journey as you prepare to create the magical taste of home and happiness with food that delights and unites hearts around your table.
