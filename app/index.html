How it Works
  • Cook Along in Kongu Style and taste the history, heritage and food culture of Kongu Nadu.
  • Annapoorna's "Cook Along in Kongu Style" is a virtual event where you are invited to join us online and cook and create Kongu Nadu's speciality dish - Nattu Kozhi Kulambu.
  • Join us from your kitchen to participate in this cook along. Whether you are a chef, home cook, foodie or food lover, you can cook up a delicious dish from wherever you are.
  • To participate, register for the cook along by filling the form with your complete details.
  • After registration, you will be receiving the complete ingredients list and the recipe instructions to your email address a week before the cook along event.
  • This tasty event will take place on 7th February 2021 from 12 noon to 1 pm. During the event, you will be guided with step-by-step instructions as we cook together.
  • Cook along with us and share your slurp moments by tagging and sharing it on our social channels. Follow us on Facebook, Twitter and Instagram for more tasty details.
